My background

Hi, I am Kaki. Born and raised in Hong Kong and am now based in London. After over ten years working in my home town, I took on the adventure to move to London and start my career as a UX designer.

Why User Experience

My cousin shared that she had changed her career to a UX Designer. I had never heard of this term before, and curious about what it is. After talking, I knew immediately that UX design would be my new career path. UX allows me to work on different products and projects to taste and feel different things and knowledge. I am always excited to learn new things and have an open mind.

What I bring from my previous experiences:

  • Creativity in solving the problem
  • Observation and Analytic skills in finding out the problem
  • Collaboration skills with different kinds of people
  • Curiosity about technology and human being

How would my friends describe me:

  • Proactive - in learning and exploring new things
  • Creative - to generate something new
  • Motivated - in doing what I love
  • Open-minded - to receiving others’ feedback for the improvement
  • Adaptable - flexible to a different environment
  • Integrity - to perform my best to work
  • Empathy - able to understand the feelings of anybody
